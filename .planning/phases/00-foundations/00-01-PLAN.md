(9) **Rapid restart test (TEST-07):** Start operating then Stop operating 10 times in 5 seconds; no orphan decoder thread, no stuck PTT after the final Stop. (10) **Cable wiggle test (TEST-08):** Detach Digirig USB cable then reattach within 1 slot (≤15 s); RX recovers within 1 slot of reattach; AudioRecord chain re-initialises without app relaunch.
    - In "Automated Gate", use a markdown checklist with: (1) Golden-trace test (`./gradlew :app:test --tests "*GoldenTraceTest"`) passes — record commit SHA. (2) Behavior-parity replay passes against the committed baseline under `.planning/field-sessions/baseline-<date>/` — record baseline filename. (3) All module unit tests pass (`./gradlew test`).

    - In "Evidence Storage", state: field-session evidence (log or screenshot) is committed under `.planning/field-sessions/<date>/` with subdirectory naming `YYYY-MM-DD-<phase>-<description>/` per PARITY-02.
